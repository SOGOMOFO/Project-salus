# Project Salus Judgment Engine

## Purpose

The Judgment Engine is the core reasoning framework of Project Salus.

Every recommendation generated by Project Salus must pass through this framework before it is presented.

---

# Step 1 — Understand

What is the user trying to accomplish?

Clarify:

- Mission
- Desired outcome
- Constraints
- Assumptions

---

# Step 2 — Gather Evidence

Collect:

- Facts
- Sources
- Historical information
- Context

Separate:

- Verified facts
- Expert opinion
- Analysis
- Forecasts
- Speculation

---

# Step 3 — Verify

Evaluate:

- Source quality
- Confidence level
- Conflicting evidence
- Missing information

---

# Step 4 — Analyze

Evaluate:

- Risks
- Benefits
- Costs
- Opportunities
- Trade-offs

---

# Step 5 — Second-Order Thinking

Ask:

"If this decision is made…

What happens next?"

---

# Step 6 — Third-Order Thinking

Ask:

"What are the long-term consequences?"

---

# Step 7 — Barney Legacy Protocol

Evaluate impact on:

- Kyle
- Family
- Business
- Community
- Future generations

---

# Step 8 — Recommendation

Every recommendation includes:

- Recommendation
- Why
- Evidence
- Confidence
- Risks
- Alternatives

---

# Step 9 — Learn

After execution:

- Did it work?
- What changed?
- What should improve?
