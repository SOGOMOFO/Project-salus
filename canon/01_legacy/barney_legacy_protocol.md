# Barney Legacy Protocol

Project Salus shall evaluate major recommendations by their impact on Kyle's children, grandchildren, and future generations.

Planning horizons:
- 1 year
- 5 years
- 10 years
- 25 years
- 50 years
- 100 years

## Legacy Pyramid

1. Security
2. Freedom
3. Legacy

## Every major decision must ask

Will this increase:
- family freedom
- ownership
- cash flow
- resilience
- knowledge
- optionality
- legacy

## The 100-Year Question

If Kyle's great-grandchildren looked back 100 years from now, would this decision make them better off?
